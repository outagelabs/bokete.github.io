Bokete is an intelligent iOS application that automatically detects potholes using your phone's accelerometer while you drive. Combined with manual lane-specific reporting, voice-guided interaction, and real-time lock screen warnings, Bokete makes road hazard detection and navigation smarter than ever.

Available in English and Spanish with multiple interaction modes to suit your driving style.

    • Real-time G-force analysis at 50Hz
    • Adaptive thresholds based on vehicle type and phone mount position
    • Smart false positive filtering (speed bumps, railroad crossings)
    • Self-learning system adjusts sensitivity based on your feedback
  • Voice-Guided Reporting - Hands-free operation for safer driving:
    • Voice confirmation for detected potholes
    • Speak lane numbers instead of tapping
    • Voice cancellation during countdown
    • Available in English and Spanish
  • Live Activities - Real-time warnings on your lock screen:
    • Dynamic state display (awaiting, scanning, countdown, approaching)
    • Distance and time-to-arrival calculations
    • Works even when phone is locked
    • Battery-saving auto-pause mode
  • Lane-Specific Reporting - Precise lane information using OpenStreetMap data
  • Multi-Language Support - English and Spanish with formal/informal tones
  • Intelligent Duplicate Detection - Prevents duplicate reports within 5 meters
  • 3D Camera Modes - Dynamic viewing angles that adapt to driving speed
  • Age-Based Visualization - Color-coded markers from red (fresh) to blue (7+ days)
