## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Report Information

When you submit a report (such as a traffic hazard, accident, or road condition), we collect and store:

- The type of report submitted
- The approximate location of the report
- The time the report was submitted
- Any optional text or details you choose to include

This information is used to display reports to other users and improve road awareness.

---

### 1.2 Location Information

Bokete uses location information to:
- Display your position on the map
- Enable navigation features
- Associate reports with a general location
- Enable optional location sharing with other users

Location access is controlled by your device settings.  
You may enable or disable location access at any time.

We do **not** store continuous or historical location tracking beyond what is required to support active features or submitted reports.

---

### 1.3 What We Do *Not* Collect

Bokete does **not** collect or store:
- Vehicle or car data (speed, VIN, diagnostics, fuel level, sensors, etc.)
- Driving behavior analytics
- Contacts, call logs, or messages
- Payment or financial information
- Advertising identifiers
- Biometric data

## 2. How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to:
- Operate and improve Bokete features
- Display reports and navigation-related information
- Maintain safety, security, and reliability
- Respond to user inquiries or legal obligations

We do **not** use your data for targeted advertising.

## 3. Sharing of Information

We do **not** sell your personal data.

We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

### 3.1 With Other Users
- Submitted reports may be visible to other Bokete users
- Shared location data is only shared with users you choose

### 3.2 Service Providers
We may share limited information with trusted service providers who help us operate Bokete (e.g., cloud hosting).  
They are contractually required to protect your data and use it only for our instructions.

### 3.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required to do so by law, court order, or valid legal request.

Hazard and traffic reports may be automatically deleted or anonymized after they are no longer relevant.
