HOME TERMS OF SERVICE PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: December 20, 2024

Bokete Corp ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Bokete mobile application ("App").

By using the App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

┌─ 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT ─────────────────────────────────┐

1.1 Location Data

The App collects precise location data to provide its core functionality:

  • GPS Coordinates: Latitude and longitude when reporting hazards
  • Real-time Location: Continuous tracking during navigation mode
  • Altitude: Elevation data for improved accuracy
  • Heading: Device compass direction for navigation features
  • Speed: Movement speed for driving mode detection

1.2 Report Data

When you submit a pothole or hazard report, we collect:

  • Timestamp of the report
  • Lane information (when available from OpenStreetMap)
  • Road segment details
  • Report category and severity rating
  • Accelerometer data (G-force readings for pothole detection)

1.3 Device Information

We collect basic device information:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version number
  • Unique device identifiers for crash reporting
  • Language and region settings

1.4 Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage statistics:

  • Feature usage patterns
  • App performance metrics
  • Error logs and crash reports
  • Navigation routes (anonymized)

┌─ 2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION ────────────────────────────┐

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Display hazards on the map, provide navigation, detect driving mode
  • Duplicate Prevention: Identify and merge duplicate reports within 5 meters
  • Map Enhancement: Generate heat maps and visualize road quality data
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop new features
  • Safety: Alert users to nearby hazards during navigation
  • Community Service: Share anonymized hazard data for public benefit

Note: We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

┌─ 3. DATA STORAGE AND RETENTION ─────────────────────────────┐

3.1 Local Storage

Most data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database:

  • Your submitted reports
  • Cached road information from OpenStreetMap
  • User preferences and settings

3.2 Cloud Storage

Some data may be synchronized to our secure servers:

  • Anonymized hazard reports for community access
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

3.3 Retention Period

We retain data for the following periods:

  • Hazard Reports: Indefinitely or until marked as fixed
  • Location History: Not stored beyond active session
  • Crash Logs: 90 days
  • Cached Road Data: Updated periodically, old data purged

┌─ 4. DATA SECURITY ───────────────────────────────────────────┐

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure HTTPS connections for API communications
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits and updates

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

┌─ 5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES ───────────────────────────────────┐

The App integrates with third-party services that may collect data:

5.1 Apple Maps / MapKit

  • Provides mapping and navigation functionality
  • Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

5.2 OpenStreetMap

  • Provides road geometry and lane data
  • Subject to OpenStreetMap Foundation Privacy Policy

5.3 Analytics and Crash Reporting

  • We may use services like Sentry for error tracking
  • Data is anonymized and aggregated

┌─ 6. LOCATION PERMISSIONS ───────────────────────────────────┐

The App requires location permissions to function:

  • "When In Use": Basic functionality, report submission, map display
  • "Always" (Optional): Background hazard detection and live activity updates

You can modify location permissions at any time in your device Settings. Denying location access will limit App functionality.

┌─ 7. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE ────────────────────────────┐

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Public Reports: Anonymized hazard locations are visible to all users
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Safety: To prevent harm or illegal activity
  • Business Transfer: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who assist in operating the App (under strict confidentiality)

We do NOT sell personal information to advertisers or marketing companies.

┌─ 8. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS ─────────────────────────────────────┐

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your reports and account data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Disable location tracking (with limited functionality)
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke permissions at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@bokete.app

┌─ 9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY ──────────────────────────────────────┐

The App is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@bokete.app.

┌─ 10. INTERNATIONAL USERS ────────────────────────────────────┐

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. These countries may have different data protection laws than your jurisdiction.

By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to our servers and service providers worldwide.

┌─ 11. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY ─────────────────────────────────┐

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Material changes will be communicated through the App or via email. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
